About the Collective

Flair in Cola was founded to weave a resilient, creative, and supportive network for women in Columbia’s hospitality scene. We gather to exchange craft and wisdom — over coffee, around dinner tables, and through cultivated mentorship.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We meet members where they are and lift as we climb.
  • Excellence: We foster skill, craft, and business acumen.
  • Diversity: We cultivate inclusive spaces for all backgrounds.
  • Community: We prioritize relationships and local roots.

Leadership

We are establishing a founding board of directors drawn from chefs, restaurateurs, event producers, and hospitality educators. Board roles will include President, Treasurer, Secretary, and committee leads.
