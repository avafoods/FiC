FLAIR

Where craft, care, and community meet the table.

Flair in Cola convenes women across Columbia's hospitality landscape — from restaurateurs and chefs to hoteliers and event creators — to share wisdom, mentorship, and opportunity.

Monthly Coffee & Dinner Dates

Two gatherings each month — coffee for casual connection and dinner for deeper conversations hosted at women-owned venues.

Mentorship & Workshops

Pairing emerging professionals with experienced leaders, plus masterclasses on finance, operations, and leadership.

Spotlight Local Businesses

We champion women-owned cafes, restaurants, and venues by rotating event locations each month.
